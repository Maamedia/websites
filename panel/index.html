About this panel

This panel was developed by one of the founders of Maamedia. The original panel developer is Mito, whose HTML skills are limited. And this panel is nothing special yet, but at least it makes it easier somehow. The panel gives you one-touch access to the different places Maamedia needs: like all wikis and wiki special page Special:RecentChanges, it’s important for undoing and detecting special page vandalism. In this panel, Maamedia and Mito will be happy to make suggestions for development, which you can submit to him by e-mail (link) or by leaving a message on his discussion page in Meta (link).
